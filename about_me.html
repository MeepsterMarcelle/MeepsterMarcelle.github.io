me

I'm Marcelle Weaver, a person who does digital art, design, programming for websites and videogames, and voice acting. I want to add more artforms to this list but it's what I have for now. Currently I'm doing my fourth year at UC Davis, studying Cultural Anthropology and Design. While Anthropology doesn't directly relate to artistic work it has informed my general philosophy of creativity. Like anthorpology which rides the line between the sciences and humanities, I think the most interesting work being made today is that which uses new technology to allow for new ways of expression like digital art and the constantly evolving medium of videogames. Relatedly in my work I try to begin each project with a very planned detail oriented setup which inevitably becomes warped beyond recognition during the creative proces, usually for the better.
