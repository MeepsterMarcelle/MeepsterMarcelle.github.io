about me

My artistic process focuses on creating strong composition out of 3D forms whether it be the subtle overlaps of facial structures, the organic stretch and fold of clothes, or rigid metal objects. These skills lends themself well to the sketching stages, which is why I’ve included some less polished works here.

This is based off of the botany department buildings at UC Davis, using that as a jumping off point for the whole overgrown apocalypse style. Most of the art in that theme I think fails to capture the actual messiness of abundant life and the way buildings degrade over time. Using real life inspiration to capture a more lived-in world with my art is something I do often.
